刘奕含副校长发表英文续写

作者：刘奕含



The angry wife asked him, “Where is that old thief? This is our stuff.” The porter replied: “I am not a thief, the old porter as well, the old porter is too old to carry the heavy stuff, and he was sick since last month. And he wasn’t able to earn money to have a meal of the day. He knows he was slow to deliver. So he asked for very little money. He was poor and hungry, but he was an honest man.”


Upon hearing that, the husband had tears in his eyes and turned to his wife. His wife felt so ashamed that she had no courage to look husband in the eyes. She felt sorry to the old porter. They decided to find him and apologize to he. They bought food for he and they gave the old porter and said sorry to the porter. At last, the porter took the goods to their house, and the rich couple changed their attitude to the porter.
